  • Library notifications are by e-mail only from February 1st, 2012

    2012-01-03 - http://www.kb.dk//en/REX/sider/emails.html Starting February 1st, all regular recall letters, hold notifications, and reminders from The Royal Library / Copenhagen University Library and Information Service will be sent as e-mail only.

  • Starting January 1st, 2012, you will also need to log on to use licensed resources at University of Copenhagen locations

    2011-12-20 - http://culis.ku.dk/news/eresslogin/

  • Mobile REX

    If you access www.kb.dk/rex on your smartphone, you'll now be directed to a mobile-friendly version of REX.

  • New service recommends research articles

    When you find articles using REX, a new tab called "Recommendations" suggests articles that may be relevant for your research. The recommendations provided are based on the analysis of millions of transactions performed by researchers worldwide.
